About this site

Want to know more about the site's history? We started out around mid-2000 as a small homage to the Mega Man and Mega Man X gaming series. I used the site as a playground for learning HTML and had a blast learning new skills and putting them to use in the many incarnations of the site (this current one is version 7).

I found my niche in the community for building detailed strategy guides for a handful of the Mega Man games, and ended up working on other sites in a similar capacity since then. So if you ever need a hand taking down Dr. Wily's Robot Masters or tackling Sigma and his Mavericks, have a browse through our guides to hopefully find the answer.

We also once had close ties to Planet Mega Man, a former Mega Man-powerhouse fan site. As of 2014 there's still a few fan sites from the early days, and even with the departure of the legendary Keiji Inafune from Capcom (one of the lead designers of the series), it's great to see people still enjoying the legacy games.
